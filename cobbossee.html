Restoring a century of history on the Maine lake.

Cobbossee Cabins is a careful restoration of three structures originally built around 1910 on a Maine lake. The project honors the character and craftsmanship of the original buildings while bringing them into contemporary use.

The work involved structural repair, envelope improvements, and thoughtful interior upgrades — all carried out with sensitivity to the historic fabric and the tranquil lakeside setting.
