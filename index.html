The Practice

Dedicated to architectural and interior design and to the preservation of the natural world.

Auerbach Ottinger Architects serves residential, museum, and commercial clients across Martha's Vineyard, Cambridge, New York City, the Hamptons, Vermont, and Maine.

Our practice is guided by a belief that architecture should honor its site, serve its inhabitants, and tread lightly on the land.
