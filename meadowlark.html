Project Details
  • LocationRegional
  • StatusDocumentation Phase
  • TypeResidential Expansion
  • BuiltOriginal structure, c. 1980s

Expanding a home in dialogue with its history.

Meadowlark is a thoughtful expansion of a 1980s residence, designed to extend the home's program while respecting the character and spatial logic of the original structure. New and existing elements are brought into conversation through careful material and formal choices.

The project is currently in the documentation phase.
